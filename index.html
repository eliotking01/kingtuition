About Me

I am a First-Class BSc Economics graduate from the University of Bath, having previously achieved four A* grades in my A-Levels. Alongside my studies, I have also gained over 12 months experience in London working for a Hedge Fund, and a FinTech startup helping underserved customers gain access to credit.

None of this would have been possible without the tuition I received in preparation for my 11+ entrance exam. My tutor supported me where my state school could not, enabling me to access a high-quality Grammar School Education which proved invaluable in achieving my goals. Now, I'd like to give back to ensure students receive the same opportunities as myself.

Experience

Over the past two years, I have taught over 500 hours of Economics & Maths lessons at both GCSE and A-Level, as well as preparing students for the 11+ exam. During this time I have become proficient in using online classrooms to ensure students get the most out of their time with me.

I pay close attention to each student's respective exam board. I dedicate time to explore the specification / mark schemes and adapt lessons to ensure the content and techniques I teach are specific to the requirements of the student's relevant exam board.

I provide thoroughly planned yet flexible lessons, identifying which skills students need to develop, providing well-explained examples, and using plenty of practice questions.
